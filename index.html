Ernest: Finance App with an AI-Adviser

Tools: Figma, Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, OpenAI, Premiere Pro, After Effects

I designed an iOS-native finance app that helps users save for meaningful goals by transforming complex financial data into clear, actionable guidance. I led the end-to- end product design process, from competitive analysis to user research to personas, flows, mock-ups, and usability testing. Crafting a guided onboarding experience, real- time spending insights, and AI-supported personalized plans. My focus was on reducing overwhelm, building trust and empowering users to make confident financial decisions without prior expertise.
